Lion’s Mane

Mature sample:

Lion’s Mane grows in a fluffy and interestingly patchy manner. Rhizomorphs spread out and then release clumps of fluffy mycelium which eventually grow together.

This species produces mushroom primordia ( a.k.a. “pins” which will eventually grow into mature mushrooms ) quite easily on agar.

on potato dextrose Agar

Inoculated with a piece of agar with live mycelium.

Incubated @ 25 Celsius for 7 Days:

Growth proceeds slowly with this species.
